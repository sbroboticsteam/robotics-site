Frequently Asked Questions

 What does the robotics team do?

Glad you asked! Our goal is to design robots for various purposes – to solve certain real-world issues, compete in robot competitions, and more! As an academic club, we strive to help bolster members’ engineering skills by providing an outlet through which their abilities and knowledge can be applied. In addition to building robots as a team, we hold mentorships, where students can work one-on-one with team leads to complete personal projects. The team also hosts social and professional events designed to gather students together and prepare them for future internship/career opportunities!

 Are there any requirements to join the team?

Any and all Stony Brook students, regardless of their major, year and/or experience level can join the robotics team.

 As a member, what can/must I do?

You can contribute as much (or as little) as you wish – you aren’t obligated to do any level of work! Additionally, the work you put in can be in different forms, whether it’s working on the team’s current main project, assisting at events, etc.

The robotics team is composed of three main engineering groups: mechanical, electrical, and software. The mechanical team is often responsible for the structural design of the robots (i.e. CAD work, assembly). The electrical team works to find efficient power solutions for the robots (i.e. circuitry). The software team handles the controls of the robot by creating/implementing code. You can join any of these groups, and even be involved in more than one!

 What will I gain from joining the robotics team?

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that requires collaboration between people with diverse skills, from mechanical fabrication to computer vision. Regardless of what you are passionate about and what you want to achieve, there will be ample opportunity to take on interesting challenges and learn something new.

Joining our Business Endeavors team will help you gain marketing and event management skills.


 How do I contact the robotics team?

You can contact us through our email: robotics_team@stonybrook.edu

  Can I join the Stony Brook Robotics Team if I have no experience?

Absolutely! We are dedicated to providing our members with skills and experience necessary to succeed.

Our mentorship program, workshops, and annual Internal Competition will help you get started by teaching you the fundamentals of robotics, engineering, and more!

 What if I am not ready to commit to the Stony Brooks Robotics Team this semester?

No problem! We are always looking for passionate and dedicated students to join us. You can generally join us and get involved during any semester.

We will also be hosting a series of introductory robotics workshops throughout the year. This may be a good opportunity to learn more and see if robotics is the field for you.

  What is the time commitment necessary to be part of the Stony Brook Robotics Team?

While we do not have any time commitment requirements, we encourage all of our members to dedicate as much as they feel is necessary to accomplish their tasks.

All of our projects happen because our dedicated members are excited and passionate about the work that they do. We hope that you too will feel the same about the projects that you participate in.

  Can I be part of multiple engineering teams, such as electrical and mechanical, or multiple projects
at the same time?

Certainly! While we do encourage our members to take their workload into account before making a commitment, you have the freedom to be part of as many engineering teams and projects as you wish, if you feel you are capable of making the necessary time commitment.
