PARTNER
WITH US

We are a team of 50+ students from a wide variety of disciplines, from atmospheric sciences to electrical engineering. By supporting the Stony Brook Robotics Team, you are aiding in the inspiration and training of the next generation of roboticists, scientists and engineers. Partnering with SBRT is a great way to increase your reach to Stony Brook students, hire potential recruits, and obtain international exposure when we proudly wear your logo at our competitions!

All partners are prominently featured on our website and apparel, all of which help broadcast your name to the Stony Brook community. Our different levels of partnership provide companies a cost-effective way to support the team, with different levels of partnership corresponding to respective levels of promotion.

If you are interested in partnering with Stony Brook Robotics Team, please reach out to us.
